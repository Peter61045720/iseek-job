person_add Account Registration Users, Contacts

Users can create a personal account through a standard registration process. Contacts are required to register a company alongside their personal account.

manage_accounts Manage Account Users, Contacts

Both Users and Contacts can edit some of their account details and also have the option to delete their account.

 business_center Viewing Job Offers Users

Users can browse available job offers, use the search bar to find specific positions, and apply to jobs directly through the platform.

 apartment Viewing and Editing Company Details Contacts

Contacts can view and edit the details of their associated company.

 post_add Posting Job Offers Contacts

Contacts can post, edit, and delete job offers associated with their company.

 checklist Managing Job Applications Contacts

Contacts can update the status of received applications, including accepting or rejecting candidates.

 group_add Registering a New Contact Contacts

An existing contact can register additional contacts under the same company.
